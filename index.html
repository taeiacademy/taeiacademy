The core thesis

You are not
overwhelmed.
You are patterned.

"Overwhelm is the symptom.
The pattern is the structure."

Most people aren't choosing their life. They're repeating it. High-functioning does not mean aligned. The exhaustion you feel isn't from doing too much, it's from carrying scripts you never consciously chose. Until you can see the pattern, you cannot interrupt it.

How you parent
The way you were raised becomes the default script for how you raise others, until you interrupt it.
How you partner
Relational patterns repeat across relationships. Not because you choose them, because you haven't seen them yet.
How you perform
High performance can be a script too. Productivity as avoidance. Achievement as identity. Until it isn't sustainable.

Common questions

What people ask
before they arrive.

Is this therapy?

No. Off Script is not therapy and does not replace it. This is a structured experiential workshop focused on pattern awareness, not clinical treatment. If you are currently in therapy, this work is complementary, not competing.

Who is this for?

High-functioning professionals who feel like something is off but can't name what. People who have tried other personal development programs and still feel stuck. You don't need to be in crisis to come. You need to be willing to look.

What actually happens in the room?

We don't publish the full agenda intentionally , the experience depends on not knowing what's coming. What we can tell you: no group therapy, no forced vulnerability, no scripted breakthroughs. Just structured, honest work.

Why RM 500?

RM 500 is our early bird rate, available until 30 June 2026. After that, tickets move to RM 750. This is the first time Off Script runs in Malaysia. The price reflects the depth of the work, not a mass-market seminar.

Is this religious or spiritual?

No. TAEI's work is grounded in behavioral psychology and NLP, not religion or spirituality. People of all backgrounds and beliefs attend. The work is about patterns, not prescriptions.

What happens after the workshop?

Attendees get access to the TAEI community and early access to our 6–8 week program launching later in 2026. The workshop is the door. The program is the excavation.
